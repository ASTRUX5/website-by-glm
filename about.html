Our story so far

2013

Started with a single listing on the boulevard and a belief that Kashmir deserved better than classified ads.

2015

Placed our fiftieth family. Word of mouth became our only marketing.

2018

Expanded to Gulmarg and Pahalgam. Started curating mountain properties.

2020

The year everything paused. We used it to deepen relationships, not chase numbers.

2023

Two hundred properties. Twelve years. Still driven by the same feeling — that Kashmir changes people.

2025

Today. Still here. Still believing that the right home finds you, not the other way around.
